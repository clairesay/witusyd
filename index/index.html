Women in Technology aims to focus on the holistic experience of students interested in technology by facilitating a combination of social, professional development and educational events. Through these events and on-going engagement, our members are exposed to opportunities, internships and graduate programs. We work to provide students with steppingstone into a career in tech while also expanding the strong network of females in the industry.
